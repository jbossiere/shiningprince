About

Consulting services company specializing in distribution and marketing of theatrical, home media and digital filmed entertainment. Japan & Asia focused. Experience in managing and developing theatrical distribution operations, home media distribution operations, and online on-demand digital streaming services. Also specialize in market entry for new entertainment related products & services.

We have been advancing the business interests of clients in Asia since our founding in 2004 with a proven track record of success.
